CV


Samantha Leff is a sound designer based in Mexico City. Her work encompasses projects in audio for film, television, and dubbing, as well as independent pieces and live performances. She has collaborated with filmmakers, musicians, audio engineers, writers, and visual artists, among others. Her approach emphasizes the manipulation of recorded and found sounds, as well as modular synthesis and the creation of auditory compositions using coding and diverse software tools. Exploration remains central to her practice, marked by constant experimentation with new instruments, sonic sources, and mediums
